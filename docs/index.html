I play in the digital space to produce goods such as visual art and games.

My Process:

  • Every day I wake up amazed the world exists.
  • I am grateful that I have been given the gift of a day.
  • I have coffee, and chase my muse with as much passion as my caffeine driven being allows.
  • I have more coffee, and refine the raw nature of what I found during the chase, until I have something as fine as I can make it.
  • I offer those refined goods back into the world as thanks.
  • I gain feedback to improve.
  • I relax and enjoy life, usually with a bit of chamomile tea, and occasionally with some dark chocolate.
  • Repeat.
